About

How to Use

To use this tool please type in the agency of interest into the search bar and hit the 'List' button to generate a list of the corresponding offices or the 'Map' button to generate points matching the corresponding offices on the map. If you would like all PG County Offices please hit the 'Get All Offices' button.

Purpose

Many students and faculty coming to the University of Maryland, College Park are unfamilar with PG County. On top of the many stresses of being in an academic environment, finding the nearest PG County Agency to solve their problems can be a daunting task. To solve this problem I created the PGC Agency Finder. It displays a list of offices depending on the agency the user is interested in and can display each office on a map centered on UMD.
